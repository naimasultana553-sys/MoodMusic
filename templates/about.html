About The Project

Learn how we use Artificial Intelligence to understand your emotions and connect you with the perfect music.

What is Machine Learning?

Machine Learning (ML) is a subset of Artificial Intelligence that enables systems to learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed.

In this project, we use Natural Language Processing (NLP), a branch of ML that focuses on the interaction between computers and human language, allowing our application to "read" and understand the sentiment behind your words.

The Algorithm

While this is a demonstration interface designed to connect to various backends, a typical implementation uses algorithms like Multinomial Naive Bayes, Support Vector Machines (SVM), or modern Transformer models (like BERT).

The model is trained on thousands of text samples labeled with specific emotions, allowing it to recognize patterns and vocabulary associated with different moods.

Project Workflow

1. Input

User provides text describing their current state of mind.

2. Processing

Text is cleaned, tokenized, and vectorized (TF-IDF) by the backend.

3. Prediction

The ML model analyzes vectors and predicts the dominant emotion.

4. Recommendation

Songs matching the predicted mood are retrieved and displayed.
